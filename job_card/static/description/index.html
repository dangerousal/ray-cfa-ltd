Job Card

Job Card for Worker / Technician / Users / Machine Operator.

This app allow you to have job card with all the details related to that job along with instructions to do job. Your Worker / Technician / Users / Machine Operator / All Types of Job Workers can access job card in Odoo and see details and instructions. They can also fill Timesheet in time card tab available on Job card.

Main Features:

  • Job Card form and list view for your users/operators.
  • Wokshop Team on Job Card.
  • Quality Checklists on Job Card.
  • Instructions on Job Card.
  • Costsheet on Job Card.
  • Job Card on Material Requisition.
  • Material Requisition on Job Card.
  • Job Invoice Line on Job Card.
  • Timesheet on Job Card.
  • Print the pdf report for Job Card and Costsheet on Job Card.

General Workflow:

  • Project Manager / Supervisor can Configure the Quality Checklists and Workshop Team and Instruction on Job Card.
  • Project Manager / Supervisor can create the Job Card.
  • Project User / Operator view/add the instruction, costsheet, material requisition, requistion line, job invoice line, timesheet on Job Card.
  • Project Manager create the customer invoice from job invoice line on Job Card.
